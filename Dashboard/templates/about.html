Jankuruma

What does "Jankuruma" mean exactly? pretty much nothing. To tell you the truth, its two words mashed together. "Janku" that means junk in japanese. And "Kuruma" which means car. If you are an enthusiast of old cars and still know how to drive a manual transmission this is the place for you!

My name is Keith and I have had a passion for older things in general but to see an older car on the road was like looking into a time machine. Back when things were a whole lot simpler. And COOLER!
